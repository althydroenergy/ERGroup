<h1>Extremal Froude Number Fr ≈ 0.3094</h1>

<p>
The <b>extremal Froude number</b> 
<b>Fr ≈ 0.3094</b> was derived analytically from an energy balance
for an ideal, inviscid open-channel flow with energy extraction.
</p>

<p>
This result is fundamentally different from the classical critical condition
<b>Fr = 1</b> and corresponds to the maximum extractable power
within the subcritical regime.
</p>
