Extremal Froude Number Fr ≈ 0.3094

This page presents the derivation and statement of the extremal Froude number

Fr ≈ 0.3094

The value arises from energy optimization in ideal open-channel flow without friction or losses. It represents a fundamental limit analogous in role to the Betz limit for wind turbines, but applicable to free-surface flows.

Key Result

For an ideal incompressible fluid in open-channel flow, the extractable energy reaches a limit at the extremal Froude number:

Frext ≈ 0.3094

This value is obtained analytically from the energy balance and does not depend on empirical resistance laws (e.g., Manning or Chezy).
