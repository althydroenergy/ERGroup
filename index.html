Extremal Froude Number Fr ≈ 0.3094

The extremal Froude number corresponding to the energy extremum in open-channel flow is:

Fr ≈ 0.3094

This value is obtained analytically from first principles under ideal-flow assumptions and represents a universal limit for energy extraction in open-channel and tidal flows.
