Innovative Small Hydropower – ERGroup

Damless • Continuous • Renewable

A next-generation free-flow micro-hydropower solution designed to generate clean electricity 24/7 from rivers, canals and slow watercourses — without dams.

Technology Overview

ERGroup develops free-flow micro-hydropower turbines capable of operating in low-velocity watercourses. Unlike conventional hydropower systems, this technology does not require dams, large head differences or complex civil infrastructure.

The system is modular, scalable, and suitable for rural electrification, agricultural infrastructure, and decentralized power supply.

Key Advantages

✔ No Dams Required

No flooding, no major environmental disruption.

✔ 24/7 Operation

Continuous energy production independent of sunlight or wind.

✔ Low Installation Cost

Minimal civil works and simplified deployment.

✔ Scalable Design

Modular units for small communities and agricultural use.

✔ Environmentally Friendly

Reduced ecological impact compared to traditional hydropower plants.

✔ High Extraction Efficiency

Optimized flow conditions improve energy capture performance.
