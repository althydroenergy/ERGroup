ERGroup: Innovative Small Hydropower Project

ERGroup is engineering a new generation of free-flow, highly efficient micro-hydropower stations designed to capture clean electricity from natural water flow without dams or large civil works. 1

Our project focuses on developing and mass-producing small hydroelectric power units that can be installed in rivers and canals to provide sustainable, decentralized energy for farms, villages, and remote communities. 2

Project Vision

The goal of the ERGroup initiative is to create a cost-effective, scalable micro-hydropower platform using innovative turbine designs that make renewable energy accessible and practical in settings where traditional hydroelectric power is not feasible. 3

The system is engineered to operate directly in flowing water, capturing kinetic energy efficiently while minimizing ecological disruption. This approach supports climate-friendly electrification and resilience of electric supply in rural and off-grid regions. 4

Features & Benefits

  • Free-flow operation — no dams or large infrastructure required. 5
  • Micro-scale hydropower suitable for farms, industries, and small communities. 6
  • Potential to significantly lower energy costs and increase reliability in underserved areas. 7
  • Supports sustainable development through renewable energy adoption. 8
