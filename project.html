Scientific Background

The theoretical foundation of the project is described in the research work on energy extraction limits in open-channel flow.

The analysis shows that optimal energy extraction occurs near the dimensionless Froude number:

Fr ≈ 0.3094

Under these conditions the theoretical maximum energy extraction approaches approximately 34.52% of the flow energy.
