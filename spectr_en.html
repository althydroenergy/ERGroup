Full English Translation of the Spectr-Plus Interview

An engineer from Tashkent is convinced that he has discovered a physical process that appears to conflict with certain established interpretations of physics, and whose development and implementation may help humanity address the urgent global energy crisis.

Uzbekistan’s history has known many gifted scientific pioneers whose names became recognized far beyond the country’s borders. Perhaps history is repeating itself. Our protagonist, German Treshchalov, an energy engineer by profession, has already filed applications with local and international patent offices and built a working prototype based on the principle of his discovery.
