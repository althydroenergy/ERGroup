Frequently Asked Questions

Why is your unit more favorable than solar panels? ▶
Our water turbine is even cleaner and cheaper than solar energy because it does not depend on weather conditions, time of day, or season and can operate 24/7 at full capacity.
Last update: 09.03.2024
Is it environmentally friendlier than solar panels? ▶
Hydraulic turbines do not require consumable components such as batteries or solar panels that degrade over time. Their production and disposal therefore have a smaller environmental impact.
Last update: 09.03.2024
What is the power capacity of your turbine? ▶
The smallest turbine unit has a capacity of approximately 3 kW and can generate more than 70 kWh of electricity per day, enough to power two or three houses.
Last update: 12.03.2024
Can one turbine supply electricity to a farm or village? ▶
Large units may reach 200–300 kW. Such installations could supply electricity to farms, villages, or small settlements.
Last update: 09.03.2024
Will the turbine harm fish? ▶
The turbine rotates slowly (20–30 rpm) and does not block the full channel width. For fish it behaves like a natural obstacle and even helps aerate the water.
Last update: 13.03.2024
